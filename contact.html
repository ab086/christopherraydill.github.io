Contacting Us Directly

Are you using mods?

We have no problem with people modding our games, but please be aware that you use mods on an "At your own risk" basis. If you're experiencing game crashes or freezes with mods installed, you need to troubleshoot what mods could be causing the issue and contact those authors. We cannot help you.


If you're not using mods

If the FAQ page was unable to answer any of your questions, then you may contact us directly to receive specialized support. We work hard to make sure that you don't experience any problems, but they do happen occasionally. Before you submit your ticket, make sure that the email address is valid and that you can check it!

Tickets can take 24-48 hours to receive a reply. Duplicate tickets can slow this process down, so be sure you're only submitting the ticket once. When you contact support, please be sure to give us...

  • Your account name
  • Your "log.txt" file if you're experiencing game crashes or freezes
  • As much information as possible on the issue

We will NEVER ask you for...

  • Your password
  • Your credit card information

Receiving any of the above mentioned informaton will result in your ticket being deleted.
